WELCOME TO Coder puzzle


Well CODER PUZZLE is very easy to play. The letters are shuffled, you have to rearrange the letters to form the corresponding word, input it and use the check button or hit enter to check if your guess is correct. You can also use the space key to shuffle the given letters. by playing this game it helps your IQ to know the basics as a developer...
Click on CODER puzzle in the big circle to start all over

Example:

Shuffled Word: M R E G A

Correct Word: G A M E R
